Banana Cream Pie

Description

Creamy and smooth, this banana cream pie is sure to be a family favorite. Ready to serve in about 2 hours and easy to prepare ahead of time, this pie is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3 egg yolks, beaten
  • 4 bananas, sliced
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine sugar, flour, and salt in a saucepan
  3. Slowly add milk
  4. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened then remove from heat
  5. In a medium bowl, whisk egg yolks and a small amount of hot milk until smooth
  6. Slowly add egg mixture to the saucepan
  7. Cook over medium low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture coats the back of a spoon
  8. Cook for 2 more minutes, then remove from heat
  9. Fill pie crust with sliced bananas and pour pudding mixture overtop
  10. Bake at 350 degrees F until filling sets (approx. 12 to 15 minutes)
  11. Let cool to room tempurature on a wire rack
  12. Chill in refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving
