Pecan Pie

Description

Looking for something sweet? This pecan pie is sure to delight. This southern style pie is simple, delicious, and can be baked in about one hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups chopped pecans
  • 1/4 cup dark corn syrup
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 Tbsp cold water
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In a saucepan, combine sugar, dark corn syrup, water, cornstarch, and butter
  3. Place sauce pan over medium heat until boiling, then remove from heat
  4. With an electric mixer, beat eggs in a large bowl until frothy
  5. While mixing, slowly add cooked syrup
  6. Stir in salt, vanilla, and pecans
  7. Pour filling into pie crust
  8. Bake at350 degrees F until filling is set (approx. 45 to 50 minutes)
  9. Let cool before serving
